# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 24, 2026

GetNayi (owned and operated by Aisles Platform, based in the United States) values your trust. When you use our application to discover and browse creator items, we gather specific categories of information.

## 1. Personal Data We Collect
* **Account Credentials:** Email addresses and securely hashed password records collected during signup.
* **Profile Metrics:** Custom display names, bio descriptions, social links, and optional profile avatars.
* **Device & Interaction Logs:** IP addresses, browser user-agent profiles, operating system indicators, page navigation behaviors, and video playback engagement metrics.
* **Dynamic Settings:** Bookmarked videos, curated shopping collections, and dark/light mode preference states.

*Note: We do not collect or view your raw password keys; these are processed securely before persistent storage using bcrypt-12.*

## 2. Why We Collect Your Information
* **Service Execution:** Registering your account, authenticating sessions, loading custom video feeds, and managing your bookmark collections.
* **Product Optimization:** Evaluating user retention, identifying system bottlenecks, enhancing video player latency, and troubleshooting responsive features.
* **Fraud Protection & Security:** Thwarting malicious bot crawls, monitoring secure login attempts, and implementing security rate-limiting.

## 3. Who We Share Your Data With
We never sell your behavioral analytics or profile data to brokers or third-party advertisers. We only share specific data structures with highly secure, industry-standard service providers:
* **Stripe:** Payment credentials and subscription billing data. GetNayi never holds or stores raw credit card details on our servers.
* **Google Cloud & Firebase:** Secure cloud storage facilities for user-generated metadata.
* **Google Analytics:** Tracks anonymized viewport interactions. All user IP addresses are heavily anonymized.
* **Google OAuth Services:** Facilitates standard single sign-on access parameters.

## 4. Hashing & In-Depth Protection
Raw text passwords undergo a salt layering process on the backend utilizing the standard bcrypt-12 formula. All active connections enforce standard TLS/HTTPS encryption to secure information during transport.

## 5. Data Retention Durations
* **Account Metadata:** Stored permanently while your account remains active.
* **Interaction & Device Metrics:** Auto-purged from Google Analytics records after 14 months of storage.
* **Accounting and Payment Records:** Stored for up to 7 years to meet strict local tax and auditing mandates.

## 6. Your Rights, Deletion, and Data Export
Regardless of your physical location, GetNayi supports complete transparency and individual control under GDPR and CCPA rules. You have the right to request a comprehensive copy of all your profile content or request a complete, irreversible account purge.
